Project: Investigate a TMDB Movie Dataset¶

Introduction¶

We are going to explore together a TMDB movie dataset. This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDB), including user ratings and revenue. Personally, I never looked at the movies from a prespectve such as - what makes a movie achieve a high revenue? So, it is a good chance to figure it out and get an impression of the film industry.

We will first start with a general exploration of the data, to see trends . Then, we are going to research the following:

  • Exlpore the things that affect the revenue, by looking at a couple of independent variables like budget, vote_average etc.
  • Focus on the actors, and explore things like actors occurrence in the dataset and the presentage of actors who happened to be directors as well.

Columns to drop¶

Here, I am dropping the columns that don't serve me any purpose in the analysis. For revenue and budget, I am taking the adjusted one. I've compared the two columns (the adjusted and the normal one) and noticed the following:

  • Revenue numbers are from 2010 ( source: kaggle)
  • Revenue adjusted value is greater than the original revenue value the following years
    • 1977
    • 2009
  • Revenue adjusted value is lesser than the original revenue value for the following years
    • 2014
    • 2015
  • Revenue adjusted value is equal to the original revenue value (revenue column) for the following:
    • 2010 -This tells us that the data was collected in 2010.

So I decided to use the adjusted values and drop the orginal one

Conclusion¶

The movie dataset has endless ways of interpretation. However, missing values for important variables like budget and revenue makes it hard to get an insight. It gives us an insight into the direction that needs to be explored in the future.

We saw that, on average, movies were profitable (keep in mind the missing values). The text information in this data is very rich, as we saw in the case of the casts. It would make a strong case for a machine learning case study. An interesting direction I would like to pursue is the relation between movie revenues and the casts/directors.

Finally, the analysis that was performed shows "an example" of what can be done with such a dataset.
